Welcome to Eduflare

Eduflare presently in incubator, starts its journey in 26th day of June,2024,have emerged from an East based skill building organization, with a view to become one of the top 10 skill building and workforce development company of India. The right mix of knowledge partnership with industry leaders like Microsoft in specific industry & the standardization of delivery methodology from mission SKILL INDIA and different Sector Skill Councils (SSC), Eduflare will become a trusted skilling partner of Govt. of India and most of the States Govt.

Eduflare's mission is to implement ground breaking methodologies in delivering industry specific IT/non-IT solutions that answers major business challenges & empower the youth for greater heights tomorrow. Eduflare achieves its mission using it's technological expertise, collaborative approach & passionate team of professional.
