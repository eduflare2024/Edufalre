Course Overview

The J2EE course is designed for developers who want to master the Java 2 Enterprise Edition platform and build scalable, robust, and enterprise-level applications. This course covers the core components of J2EE, including Servlets, JSP (JavaServer Pages), EJB (Enterprise JavaBeans), and more. It also explores best practices for developing and deploying enterprise applications, ensuring that you are equipped to handle real-world challenges in a professional environment.
